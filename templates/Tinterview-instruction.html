Instructions

1) Find a quiet and well-lit room where you won't be disturbed during the interview.


2) Wear earphones to minimize background noise and ensure better sound recording.


3) Read each question carefully. Take your time to understand the problem and requirements.


4) Maintain eye contact with the camera as much as possible to simulate a real interview environment.


5) Once you've completed your answer, click on the "Next Question" button to proceed to the next question.


6) Select the technical topics you'd like to be tested on before starting the interview. You'll receive 5 questions for each selected topic.


7)When you're ready to begin the interview, click on the "Start Interview" button.


Relax and do your best! Mock interviews are a valuable opportunity to practice and improve your interviewing skills.
